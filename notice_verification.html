Notice Verification

SNVP recognizes that reputation and trust in the industry are of the utmost importance.

The Document Verification Service provides one way for you to verify the authenticity of payment notices sent by your respective fund manager.

To verify payment notices, simply input the Notice ID and Provided Bank No. that is included in every payment notice. Our systems will check and validate the Notice ID against the blockchain records.
