Our Features

1) Secure Notification Verification

SNVP offers an all-encompassing platform for investors to check the authenticity of their investment funds notifications; utilizing blockchain technology and open banking API technology to swiftly and securely validate investment fund notifications.

2) Convenient Payment Options

SNVP offers an e-Wallet feature which allows investors to top up their SNVP and automate the payment process. Investors can set up auto-payment; where the amount owing for payment into their fund is automatically transferred.

3) Direct Communication Channels

SNVP offers direct, secure communication channels between investors and fund managers.
